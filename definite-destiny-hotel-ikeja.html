Definite Destiny Hotel, Ikeja

Definite Destiny Hotel is located at no 4, Kofoworola Street, Balogun, Ikeja, Lagos. It is a 5-minute drive from Murtala Mohammed International Airport and a 3-minute drive from Ikeja mall. kalakuta museum is 201m away from Definite Destiny Hotel. It is a hotel that strongly in bringing happiness to the lives of the clients hence the reason for building a tranquil and beautiful environment for our clientele. It offers Bar/Lounge, Restaurant, Shower, Refrigerator, swimming pool, Adequate parking space, airport pickup, security, king sized bed and free Wi-Fi.
Each room is fully air-conditioned and is fitted with a TV and a DVD, with satellite and it also comes with a private bathroom fitted with a bathtub or shower. There are also telephones, refrigerator and a wardrobe in each with a water heater. There is also free shuttle service at the property.
The guests enjoy complimentary dinner on the day of arrival and free breakfast every other day. Laundry service and car hire service are also available for use. Hotel policies include;
Check in: from 2:00 pm. ID required.
Check out: by 12:00 pm.
Pets: they are not allowed.
Accepted credit cards: Visa, the hotel reserves the right to pre-authorize credit cards prior to arrival.
Children and Extra beds: children cannot be accommodated at the hotel, there is no capacity for extra beds in the room.
Cancellation and payment vary according to room type. Please check what room conditions may apply when selecting your room option.
