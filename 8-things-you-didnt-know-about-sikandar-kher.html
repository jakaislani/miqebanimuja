8 Things You Didnt Know About Sikandar Kher

➡ Sikandar Kher Biography

Sikandar Kher, an Indian actor who portrayed the role of Rama Shetty in ‘Mumbhai’ which is Zee5’s original project. The actor has worked in several films including ‘Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey’, ‘Players’, ‘Aurangzeb’, ‘The Zoya Factor’, and many others that are worth sharing to watching. Well, the brilliant actor has the ability to win thousands of hearts with his outstanding performances. However, many of us are aware of his personal ad professional life, but still, some of his fans are unaware and want to know more about him. Here is a quick look at some unknown things they probably did not know.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Sikandar Kher

1- Setep-son of Anupam Kher

Sikandar Khan is the step-son of the veteran, famed Bollywood actor Anupam Kher. The actor was first offered the role of Sameer in the film ‘Dil Chahta Hai’ released in 2001, but later, Saif Ali Khan was chosen to play the role of Sameer.

2- Worked as Co-director

The actor completed his schooling from Dehradun and later, he joined the National School of Drama from where he did a 6-month theatre course. Thereafter, he took the start of his career as a co-director for Shahrukh Khan’s movies ‘Dil To Pagal Hai’, and ‘Devdas’.

3- Admires his Mom (Kiran Kher)

Sikandar Kher admires his mother Kiran Kher who is the leading lady. even the actor often shares lovely notes for her whom he calls his ‘Sky full of Stars’.

4- Engaged with Priya Singh

In 2016, the actor got engaged to Priya Singh who is a cousin of Sonam Kapoor. Later, their relationship did not last and they called off their engagement. After some time, Sikandar revealed that he has no regrets and it had been two years.

5- Dated Gul Panag

There were rumors speculated that Sikandar Kher dated Gul Panag who is known for appearing in ‘Rangbaaz Phirse’. However, the couple broke up in 2015.

6- Known for

The actor portrayed the role of a CBI officer on Zee5’s Original show titled ‘The Chargesheet: Innocent or Guilty. It was the first time that he played the role of CBI officer and took it as a challenge to prove himself a great actor.

7- A childhood friend of Abhishek Bachchan

Sikandar Kher and Abhishek Bachchan are close friends since their childhood. The doe also went to Actor Prepares, his father’s acting workshop with other star, such as; Hrithik Roshan, Uday Chopra, and others.

8- Love for Acting

Sikandar Kher had a love for acting since his childhood as he wanted to become an acclaimed actor. That’s the reason he used to attend such kind of acting workshops to prepare himself for the future.
