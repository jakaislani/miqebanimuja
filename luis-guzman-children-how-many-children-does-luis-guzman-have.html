Luis Guzmán is a Puerto Rican actor who has seven children with Angelita Galarza-Guzman. However, one of them passed away.

Cemi Guzman, Yoruba Briggs-Guzman, Yemaya Briggs-Guzman, Jace O’ Flynn Guzman, Luna Guzman, Clare Briggs-Guzman and Margarita Briggs-Guzman are he names of their children.

Luis Guzmán and his wife Angelita Galarza-Guzman attempted to have children after six years of marriage. Unfortunately, their first child, a son, died of suffocation at birth in 1991.

Luis Guzmán and his wife adopted their first child, Cemi, in late 1991. They later adopted another child, Yemaya, before adding half-brothers and half-sisters, the twins Yoruba and Margarita, bringing Guzman’s total number of adopted children to four.
