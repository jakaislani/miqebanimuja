Faith Nketsi Biography, Age, Boyfriend, Career &amp; Net Worth

Faith Nketsi (born 30 December 1994) is a popular South African socialite and video vixen who is popularly known as the ‘Queen of Twerk’.

She is also a model whose style and sultry selfies have earned her a massive number of fans on the internet.

Faith was also recognized as a member of the female dance crew Pro-Twerkers before she left the group to become a popular event host at nightclubs around South Africa.

The South African Instagram star also contributed to the music video titled “Tito Mboweni”. It was sung by South African rapper Cassper Nyovest.
