Every Sweet Baby Rays Sauce, Ranked [Taste Test]

The best Sweet Baby Ray’s sauce packs a huge punch of flavor when you add it to anything at all, from grilled meat to chicken nuggets. And most of them really are flavorful. It’s hard to pick a “best” Sweet Baby Ray’s sauce because they’re all (well, almost all) so dang good.

Sweet Baby Ray’s got its start at a rib cook-off in Chicago back in 1985. Chef Larry Raymond’s sauce—named after his little brother, who got the nickname playing basketball—beat nearly 700 other sauces in the competition, and the rest is history. The brand now has more than two dozen flavors of sauce that can be found in grocery stores nationwide.

For this taste test, I tried every Sweet Baby Ray’s that I was able to get my mitts on (25 of them, in fact). I drank sauce for over an hour to figure out which Sweet Baby Ray’s sauces are the sweetest, baby! Here is every Sweet Baby Ray’s sauce ranked from worst to best.
