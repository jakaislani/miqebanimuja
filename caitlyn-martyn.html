Caitlyn Martyn - InStyle

Title: Commerce Writer

Education: Lehigh University

Expertise: BeautyL, Lifestyle

  • Caitlyn Martyn joined the InStyle team as a Commerce Writer in 2022 and is responsible for writing tested product recommendations and reviews.
  • She is passionate about sharing tips she's picked up from experts as well as products that really work.

Experience

Caitlyn has experience writing and producing beauty and lifestyle content, and has tested hundreds of products over the years. Before joining InStyle, Caitlyn was the Associate Beauty Editor at L'Oréal's Makeup.com and Skincare.com, where she wrote beauty stories and learned everything there is to know about makeup, skincare and haircare.

Her work has appeared in POPSUGAR, L'Oréal Paris Beauty Magazine, The Knot and more

Education

Caitlyn earned a bachelor's degree in Journalism and a minor in Marketing from Lehigh University.
