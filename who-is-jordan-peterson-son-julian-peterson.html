Who is Jordan Peterson's Son? Meet Julian Peterson

Julian Peterson is a Canadian Software Engineer, Entrepreneur, and the Son of Jordan Peterson (Canadian psychologist, and author). Jordan Peterson is a well-known Canadian clinical psychologist, author, and public speaker who has influenced millions of people with his books, lectures, and podcasts. He is also a father of two children: Mikhaila Peterson and Julian Peterson.

While Mikhaila is a social media star and a health advocate who runs her own podcast and blog, Julian is a more private person who prefers to stay away from the spotlight. However, he is also an accomplished individual who has launched his own website and app to help writers improve their skills. In this article, we will explore the life and achievements of Julian Peterson, the son of Jordan Peterson.

Julian Peterson’s Early Life and Education

Julian Peterson was born in 1995 (Age: 28 years; as of 2023) in Canada to Jordan Peterson and Tammy Peterson (aka Tammy Roberts). He grew up in a supportive and intellectual family, where he learned the power of words and ideas from his father. He also witnessed his father’s rise to fame and controversy as he published his best-selling books 12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos and Beyond Order: 12 More Rules for Life, and became a prominent figure in the culture wars.

Julian attended the University of Toronto, where he studied computer science and philosophy. He also went to the University of King’s College. He graduated with a bachelor’s degree in 2018. He also developed an interest in writing and entrepreneurship, which led him to pursue his own projects after college.

Career and Achievements

Julian Peterson is the founder and CEO of WriteWise, a website, and app that helps writers improve their writing skills by providing feedback, suggestions, and analytics. WriteWise uses artificial intelligence to analyze the structure, clarity, coherence, style, and tone of any text and offers personalized recommendations on how to improve it. WriteWise also allows writers to compare their texts with other texts in their genre or field, and learn from the best practices of successful authors.

Julian launched WriteWise in 2020, after working on it for two years with a team of developers and linguists. He said that he was inspired by his father’s teachings on the importance of writing as a tool for thinking and communicating effectively. He also said that he wanted to create a product that would help writers become better writers, rather than just fix their grammar or spelling errors.

WriteWise has received positive reviews from users who have praised its features and functionality. WriteWise has also been featured in several media outlets, such as Hoxton Magazine, The Startup Magazine, and The Next Web. Julian said that his vision for WriteWise is to become the ultimate writing assistant for anyone who wants to write well.

Julian Peterson’s Wife: Personal Life and Interests

Julian Peterson is married to Rudy Peterson, whom he met in college. They tied the knot in September 2018, and went on a honeymoon trip to Spain. They currently live in Toronto, Canada.

Julian is active on Instagram, where he posts photos of his travels, family, friends, and hobbies. He enjoys hiking, skiing, biking, kayaking, and playing chess. He also loves reading books on various topics, such as psychology, philosophy, history, science fiction, and fantasy.

Julian has a close relationship with his father Jordan Peterson, whom he admires and respects. He often accompanies him on his tours and events and supports him through his challenges. He also has a good bond with his sister Mikhaila Peterson, who is also an entrepreneur and a podcast host.

A Son of a Legend Jordan Peterson

Julian Peterson is a son of a legend who has carved his own path in life. He is a talented writer, programmer, and entrepreneur who has created a valuable product that helps writers improve their craft. He is also a loving husband, brother, son, and friend who values his family and relationships. He is a person who lives by his own rules and strives for excellence in everything he does.
