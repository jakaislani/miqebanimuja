What Is Sofia Vergara’s Beauty Secret?

Netizens are curious about Sofia Vergara’s beauty secret, as to how she can seem that young and fresh at the age of  50 without any surgery.

Well, in an interview with Daily Mail she mentioned that she does everything healthy and follows every trend that helps her skin look fresh.

She’s always been a beauty enthusiast, wearing Creme de la Mer “since she was small” and experimenting with the latest trends.

‘People say eat chia seeds, so I do,’ she says. ‘Then there’s flaxseed, and I consume flaxseed.’ It’s quinoa now, therefore I’m eating quinoa.’

Camille Obadia anti-aging moisturizer, La Mer SPF UV Protecting Fluid, and Covergirl lipstick are among my current favorites,” she added.

However, the Colombian-born actress says that she has toned down her style since migrating to the United States.

‘I’ve developed a feeling of equilibrium,’ she explained. ‘When I was in Colombia, I would wear everything at once: a big lip, a bold eye, cleavage, a miniskirt, and heels.’

‘Because it’s more casual here, I’ll apply a modest lip with heavy liner.’ But my all-time favorite? ‘Cat’s eye.’
